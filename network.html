PROVIDERS · earn on your GPU

Rent out the card while you sleep

List any CUDA, ROCm, or Metal GPU. The coordinator routes training jobs to you by throughput and pays you per run - no identity of the submitter ever reaches your machine. You keep 70% of the job price, 85% if you stake. ● demo earnings

become a provider

$ npm i -g avecomp-worker
$ avecomp worker benchmark          # measures your GPU
$ avecomp worker start \
    --runtime cuda \
    --models aven-qwen-0.5b,aven-llama-1b \
    --account acct_you
  ● registered · idle · waiting for jobs
  ● you keep 70% (85% staked) of every run
